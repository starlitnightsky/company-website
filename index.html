Our Story

Our Story

Our CEO, who has a background as a software engineer, understands firsthand the challenges of maintaining a work-life balance in the field. Having experienced these struggles throughout many years in software development, he realized that there are many developers facing the same issues and in need of assistance.

Are you struggling to maintain a healthy work-life balance? Our platform is here to provide the support and solutions you need. We understand the pressures and demands that come with being overemployed, and we're dedicated to helping you find a better balance.

Through our platform, we offer resources, strategies, and tools to help you effectively manage your workload, prioritize your tasks, and create boundaries to protect your personal time. Our experienced team is ready to provide guidance and support tailored to your specific situation.

Take the first step towards achieving a healthier work-life balance by leveraging our platform. Let us help you regain control and find harmony in both your professional career and personal life.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Curious about what we are doing? Check Frequently Asked Questions.

  • How can you save my time?

    We are a team of dedicated engineers who specialize in providing staff augmentation service. If you want to delegate your work, we seamlessly integrate into your operations, working diligently behind the scenes to ensure the delivery of exceptional results that meet and exceed the expectations of your company or clients.

  • How does profile marketing work?

    We create a new google account for you or you create it yourself and provide us with the credentials. We then start the process to find best job opportunities and apply for you with that account. We filter the interviews you will get so you don't have to search through your email. We do provide step by step guide of what you will need to do once you choose the plan.

  • Why do you create new account?

    We use new account to simplify the process for you and us both. You won't get overwhelmed with multiple job application messages and it will be easy for us on this account to filter the important messages for you. Please note that we can only help with filtering interview emails if you do the initial setup suggested by us in the email after subscribing to the plan.

  • Should I pay enterance fee?

    No. It's completely free. You don't need to pay upfront or enterance fee. We take 20% ~ 50% of your job salary when you get a new job.

  • What job market are you targeting?

    We focus on US job market, thus for now we only support candidates who are authozied to work in the United States.
