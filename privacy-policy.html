Privacy Policy

MiracleTalents Consulting (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and handle your information, including mobile phone numbers and SMS consent.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • First and last name

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries and providing requested services
  • Communicating service-related updates and notifications
  • Sending SMS messages only when you have explicitly opted in

SMS Communications and Consent

If you choose to opt in to SMS communications, we may send you informational and service-related text messages, such as appointment updates, confirmations, or important notifications.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP.

NO SHARING OF MOBILE NUMBERS OR SMS CONSENT

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your mobile phone number or any SMS/text messaging consent information to third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

Your mobile phone number and SMS consent data are used solely for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and to deliver the services you have requested.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share personal data with trusted service providers solely as necessary to operate our business (such as website hosting or SMS delivery platforms), and only for purposes directly related to providing our services.
